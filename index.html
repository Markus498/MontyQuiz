Answer Me These Questions Three

Okay, so maybe there aren't just three questions in this quiz...that's what you get for trusting an old guy standing on a bridge.

Instructions

  • This quiz covers memorable moments from Monty Python and the Holy Grail. If you have seen the movie (and you should) you will most likely pass. If you do not pass... well, let's just say it won't be pretty.

Scoring

  • Your quiz will be scored based on how many questions you get right. The breakdown is as follows:
  • For getting none of the questions correct, you will be viciously attacked by a rabbit.
  • For getting 20% to 30% of the questions correct, a frenchman will taunt you and call your mother various names.
  • For getting 40% to 50% of the questions correct, a knight will say NI! at you.
  • For getting 60% to 70% of the questions correct, you will be serenaded by the minstrels of Sir Robin.
  • For getting 80% to 90% of the questions correct, you will arrive at Camelot.
  • For getting 90% to 95% of the questions correct, the Enchanter will awe you with his power.
  • For getting 100% of the questions correct, you will retrieve the Holy Grail and complete your quest.
